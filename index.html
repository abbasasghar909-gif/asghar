What is Quran IQ?

Quran IQ is an integrated educational framework designed to measure and develop a student's holistic connection to the Holy Quran. Unlike traditional recitation programs that focus purely on audio repetition, Quran IQ focuses on **spiritual intelligence, cognitive growth, and semantic comprehension**.

By tracking real-time vocabulary metrics (Arabic root word recognition), memory retention ratios (Hifz spaced repetition), and pronunciation precision (Tajweed articulation rules), we help students elevate their cognitive agility and deepen their spiritual reflection (Tadabbur). Whether you are teaching children or learning as an adult, increasing your Quran IQ builds life-long mental clarity and faith.
