Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 28, 2026

At Quranic IQ Academy, accessible from https://www.quraniciq.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors and students. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Quranic IQ Academy and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

When you register for a free trial class or sign up for our subscription plans, we collect the personal information you provide, including:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and WhatsApp number.
  • Schedule Preference: Day and time slots chosen, preferred instructor gender, and prior Quranic knowledge details.
  • Payment Information: All billing and secure payments are processed through Stripe; we do not store your credit card numbers on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our online classrooms.
  • Process your trial bookings and schedule 1-on-1 sessions.
  • Communicate with you via email or WhatsApp regarding class schedules, reminders, and feedback.
  • Improve and customize our vocabulary tracking metrics and learning tools.
  • Prevent fraudulent transactions and secure our portals.

3. Log Files and Cookies

Quranic IQ Academy follows a standard procedure of using log files and cookies. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected includes IP addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamps, and referring/exit pages. These are used to analyze trends, administer the site, and track user navigation on the site.

4. Third-Party Partners

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with external parties except trusted partners who assist us in operating our site and conducting our classes (such as Stripe for payments and email delivery APIs), as long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.
